## The KomiLab Project

KomiLab is a community **Engineering Intelligence Lab** — a serious technical engineering platform built for IT professionals who want to go beyond surface-level tutorials.

Every lab published on this platform is tested before publication, production-oriented with enterprise considerations, versioned with documented updates, and signed by its author with full traceability.

### Mission

Document, test, and share open-source IT architectures. KomiLab is aimed at IT engineers who build systems that need to hold up.

### Domains covered

Networking, cybersecurity, virtualization, cloud, hybrid architecture, Linux and systems, automation, monitoring, high availability, audit, and IT governance.

## Goals

The platform is designed to support multi-author contributions. If you are an IT engineer and wish to contribute, see the contact section.

## Editorial Policy

Each lab undergoes a technical review before publication. Labs with `draft: true` are not published. Major updates are documented in the lab's changelog (the `lastmod` field).

Taxonomy is controlled: no category can be created without editorial validation.
