## Data Collected

**Newsletter**: email address only. Collected with double opt-in. Hosted on MailerLite/Brevo. Deletion on request.

**Analytics**: KomiLab uses Plausible Analytics, a privacy-respecting solution (no cookies, no IP collection, GDPR compliant). No personal data is collected through analytics.

**Contact form**: data transmitted solely to respond to the request. No long-term retention.

## Your Rights

In accordance with the GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, and delete your data. For any request: contact@komilab.org.

## Cookies

KomiLab does not use third-party tracking cookies. Only a technical cookie for theme preference (dark/light) is stored locally in your browser.
